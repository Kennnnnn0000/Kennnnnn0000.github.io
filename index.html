Howard's skibidi homework

Roy isn't just a person — he's a walking plot twist. One minute he's staring into space like he's decoding the secrets of the universe, and the next he's sending you a voice message that sounds like he recorded it in a cave with a potato. He says the most chaotic things with a straight face, then acts confused when everyone else starts laughing. Somehow he always shows up at the exact wrong time and the exact right time, like he has his own personal timeline that the rest of us just have to adapt to. Hanging out with Roy feels like living in a low-budget sci-fi comedy, and honestly, life would be painfully boring without his strange energy.
